Welcome to The Warren Behrend Last Letters Webpage!

Hello Letter Viewers!

This site is dedicated to taking Warren Behrends final letters that he wrote to his family before his untimely
death, and marking them up and bringing some light to these old letters. There are two teams working on this
project and this webpage is Team 1's contributions to the project. Team 2's work can be found at a later date
when their webpage is created.

We are at Penn State Erie, The Behrend College.
